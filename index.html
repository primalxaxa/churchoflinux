A Short Manifesto

The Windows operating system has gotten progressively worse since the release of Windows 8. Microsoft has continued to make the user experience worse and their operating system more bloated with ads and unwanted programs. There IS an alternative. While Windows has spent time getting worse, Linux and their many contributors have worked tirelessly to improve their kernel and subsequent operating system. Behind us are the days of propriatary bloatware. The days of open-source computing are upon us. Let us rejoice!
